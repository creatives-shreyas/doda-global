## Section 1: Business Basics

### What is your business name?

**DODA GLOBAL PRIVATE LIMITED**

---

### What does your business do? (Describe in 2–3 sentences)

DODA GLOBAL is an agro sourcing, supply, and export company specializing primarily in coconut products along with selected spices, coffee, and tea. We work directly with farmers and sourcing networks across India to deliver premium-quality products to domestic B2B buyers and international markets. Our operations focus on reliable supply chains, quality assurance, and scalable distribution across India and global export regions.

✅ Other: Agro Products Sourcing, Supply & Export

### What is your business location(s)? (City, State, Country)

Nagpur, Maharashtra, India

---

### How long has your business been operating?

Since 2021

---

### What is your core value proposition? (Why should someone choose you over competitors?)

DODA GLOBAL operates on a direct-from-farm sourcing model, ensuring better quality control, consistent supply, competitive pricing, and traceability. We serve both international export markets and pan-India domestic supply chains with strong logistics capability and trusted partnerships with retailers and food brands.
